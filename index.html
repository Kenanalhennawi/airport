Interline Reference Notes

What this is: Partner airlines we can check in through (IATCI) or tag bags for. Through check-in = issue BP/bag tag at our counter for their flight. BP at transfer = collect boarding pass at transfer desk when required.

Separate ticket: If connecting with a partner within 24 hours on a separate ticket, baggage can be tagged through for a handling fee at check-in.

Ticketing partners (HR, GP, W2): These hold ticketing stock only — not airlines. If a ticket is issued on their stock and FZ causes disruption, we can request GP or the relevant partner to rebook non-interline onward segments.

Egypt Air (MS): Not interline. If ticket issued on GP stock and FZ disrupts, request GP to rebook MS segment.

Non-interline – no baggage through-check: British Airways (BA), Flyadeal (F3), Pegasus (PC), Pobeda (DP), Wizz Air (W6). Passengers checked in only to Dubai; valid UAE visa required; must clear immigration, collect bags, and re-check in for onward flight.

Other non-interline: Baggage through-check to final destination allowed for a fee. BP at transfer desk required.

Delay Policy Reference

ETD = Estimated Time of Departure | STD = Scheduled Time of Departure

In the event of a flight delay within 6 hours (D-6), passengers will be notified by Email/SMS as follows:

  • Departure from DXB/DWC: Passengers receive Email/SMS if delay is 30 mins or more. Report 3h before new departure (ETD-03h). Check-in closure: ETD-60.
  • Outstation (STD-based): Passengers receive Email/SMS if delay is 90 mins or more. Report 3h before original STD (STD-03h). Check-in closure: STD-60.
  • All Other Outstations: Passengers receive Email/SMS if delay is 90 mins or more. Report 3h before new departure (ETD-03h). Check-in closure: ETD-60.
